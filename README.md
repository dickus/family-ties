# Family Ties
This **RimWorld** mod tries to deepen family relationships, making parents respond to the pain and struggle of their offspring, and children worry or rejoice for their parents.

[![link](https://i.postimg.cc/QdL6n7QW/steam.png)](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3574741456)
